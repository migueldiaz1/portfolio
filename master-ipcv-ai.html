MSc in AI for Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCV-AI)

Awarded the highly competitive EMJM full scholarship to join this prestigious program, delivered by a consortium of three leading European universities.

Program Overview

The curriculum is built upon four fields: information technology, engineering, applied mathematics, and computer science. Each semester has its own objective:

  • 1st Semester (PPKE Budapest):

    Provides all students with fundamental leveraging knowledge in visual processing, numerical analysis, machine learning, high-performance hardware computing architectures, and programming methodologies.

  • 2nd Semester (UAM Madrid):

    Deepens the knowledge in visual signal processing and machine/deep learning, both in the area of DL-based IPCV and high-performance green computing.

  • 3rd Semester (UBx Bordeaux):

    Provides courses on further advances in visual signal processing and machine/deep learning, and focuses on the development of computer vision applications in different specific areas.

  • 4th Semester (Internship):

    Dedicated to an internship at a company or research laboratory, selected from our network of private- and public-sector partners. It ends with the Master thesis defense and a final oral exam.
